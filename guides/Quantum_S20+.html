#F.A.Q

Q.- ¿Why do folders in internal storage have a strange name?
A= This means that the Disable_DM-Verity was not previously installed, it is recommended to reinstall the Disable_DM-Verity and then the rom again, to avoid possible errors in storage

Q.- ¿Bixby Vision not working?
A= In the case of bixby vision you have to update first before opening the application, since it could cause unexpected closings

Q.- ¿Why does the NFC not work in variant G, and GT?
A= The answer is simple, these variants do not have the NFC Chip
